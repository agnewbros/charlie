Sync Garmin data
Optional. If you have a Garmin device, connect it to pull resting HR, sleep, Body Battery, stress, and activities automatically.
Your Garmin credentials must be stored as Vercel environment variables — they never touch the browser.

In your Vercel project → Settings → Environment Variables, add:

GARMIN_EMAIL = your@email.com
GARMIN_PASSWORD = yourpassword

Then redeploy (takes ~30 seconds). Come back here and tap Test to verify.
